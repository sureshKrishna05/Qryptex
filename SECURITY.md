# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

We actively maintain and provide security updates for the following versions:

| Version | Supported |
|--------|----------|
| 1.x    | Yes      |
| <1.0   | No       |

---

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Qryptex takes security seriously. If you believe you have identified a potential security issue, you may report it to us.

Email: team@qryptex.in  
Response Time: Within 48 hours  

Please include:
- Description of the issue  
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)  
- Potential impact  

---

## Testing Policy

Qryptex does not authorize or permit security testing, scanning, or probing of its systems by external parties.

Any form of:
- Automated scanning  
- Penetration testing  
- Exploitation attempts  

is strictly prohibited unless explicitly authorized in writing by Qryptex.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to:
- Qryptex web applications  
- APIs and backend services under our control  

---

## Disclosure Handling

If a vulnerability is reported, Qryptex will assess and take appropriate action internally. Public disclosure of any identified issue without prior written consent is not permitted.

---

## Legal Notice

Unauthorized attempts to test, scan, or access Qryptex systems may result in legal action.
